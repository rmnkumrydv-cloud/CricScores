Platform Features

Everything Cricket Needs

From registering your first match to managing a full tournament, CricScores has every tool you need.

🏏

Live Ball-by-Ball Scoring

Record every ball with Wides, No-Balls, Sixes, Fours, and wicket types. Real-time scorecard updates for every delivery of the match.

📊

Advanced Match Analytics

Track Current Run Rate, Required Run Rate, Win Probability, and projected scores. Full batting and bowling scorecards after every match.

🏆

Tournament Management

Umpires can organize full tournaments, create fixtures, manage multiple teams, and track standings — all in one place.

👤

Player Profiles & Stats

Every player gets a lifetime stats profile — runs, wickets, strike rate, economy. Build your cricketing portfolio with every match.

🔍

Discover Matches

Search for matches and tournaments by location or name. Players can find their next game and umpires can find talent from one dashboard.

🥇

Global Leaderboards

Top batsmen and bowlers ranked globally. Compete to be the best on the platform and showcase your talent to the world.
